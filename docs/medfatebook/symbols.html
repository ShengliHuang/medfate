B Symbols

The following tables list all symbols used in this document, along with their units and definition. When symbols are input for medfate model functions, the R name of those parameters in the package (either in data frame SpParamsMED, soil input data frame, or the output of functions such as spwbInput()) is also indicated.

B.1 Soils

Symbol Units R param Description
\(d_{s}\) mm widths Soil layer width in soil layer \(s\)
\(P_{clay,s}\) % clay Percent of clay in soil layer \(s\)
\(P_{sand,s}\) % sand Percent of sand in soil layer \(s\)
\(OM\) % om Percentage of organic mater per dry weight
\(BD_{s}\) \(g\cdot cm^{-3}\) bd Bulk density in soil layer \(s\)
\(P_{rocks,s}\) % rfc Percentage of rock fragment content in soil layer \(s\)
\(\theta_s\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-3}\) Volumetric moisture in soil layer \(s\)
\(\Psi_s\) MPa Water potential in soil layer \(s\)
\(\Psi_{fc}\) MPa Water potential at field capacity
\(A\), \(B\) Parameters of the Saxton pedotransfer functions
\(\theta_{sat}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-3}\) VG_theta_sat Volumetric moisture at saturation
\(\theta_{fc}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-3}\) Volumetric moisture at field capacity (-0.033 MPa)
\(\theta_{wp}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-3}\) Volumetric moisture at wilting point (-1.5 MPa)
\(\theta_{res}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-3}\) VG_theta_res Residual volumetric moisture
\(n\) VG_n Parameter of the Van Genuchten (1980) model
\(\alpha\) VG_alpha Parameter of the Van Genuchten (1980) model
\(P_{macro, s}\) % macro Percentage of macroporosity corresponding to each soil layers.
\(\gamma_{soil}\) \(mm \cdot day^{-1}\) Gsoil The maximum daily evaporation from soil
\(\kappa_{soil}\) Ksoil Extinction parameter to regulate the amount of water evaporated from each soil layer
\(WT\) mm Water table depth
\(W_{s}\) [0-1] W Proportion of volumetric moisture in relation to field capacity in soil layer \(s\)
\(S_{snow}\) mm SWE Snow water equivalent of the snow pack storage over the soil surface

B.2 Vegetation

Symbol Units R param Description
\(H_i\) cm H Total tree or shrub height of cohort \(i\)
\(CR_i\) CR Crown ratio (i.e. the ratio between crown length and total height) of cohort \(i\)
\(LAI^{live}_i\) LAI_live (Maximum) leaf area index (one-side leaf area per surface area of the stand) of cohort \(i\)
\(LAI^{dead}_i\) LAI_dead Dead leaf area index (one-side dead leaf area per surface area of the stand) of cohort \(i\)
\(LAI^{\phi}_i\) LAI_expanded Current expanded leaf area index (one-side expanded leaf area per surface area of the stand) of cohort \(i\)
\(v_{i,s}\) [0-1] V[i,s] The proportion of fine roots of cohort \(i\) in soil layer \(s\)
\(Z_{50,i}\) mm Z50 Depth above which 50% of the fine root mass of cohort \(i\) is located
\(Z_{95,i}\) mm Z95 Depth above which 95% of the fine root mass of cohort \(i\) is located

B.3 Anatomy

Symbol Units R param Description
\(H_v\) \(m^2 \cdot m^{-2}\) 1/Al2As Huber value (ratio of sapwood area to leaf area)
\(d\) \(cm\) LeafWidth Leaf width
\(SLA\) \(m^2 \cdot kg^{-1}\) SLA Specific leaf area
\(\rho_{leaf}\) \(g \cdot cm^{-3}\) LeafDens Leaf tissue density
\(\rho_{wood}\) \(g \cdot cm^{-3}\) WoodDens Wood tissue density
\(\Theta_{sapwood}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-3}\) Sapwood porosity (volume of empty spaces over total volume)
\(\Theta_{leaf}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-3}\) Leaf porosity (volume of empty spaces over total volume)

B.4 Plant hydraulics

Symbol Units R param Description
\(K_{s,max,ref}\) \(kg \cdot s^{-1} \cdot m^{-1} \cdot MPa^{-1}\) Kmax_stemxylem Maximum stem sapwood reference conductivity per leaf area unit
\(K_{r,max,ref}\) \(kg \cdot s^{-1} \cdot m^{-1} \cdot MPa^{-1}\) Kmax_rootxylem Maximum root sapwood reference conductivity per leaf area unit
\(k_{s, max}\) \(mmol \cdot s^{-1} \cdot m^{-2} \cdot MPa^{-1}\) Maximum whole-stem conductance (per leaf area unit)
\(k_{r, max}\) \(mmol \cdot s^{-1} \cdot m^{-2} \cdot MPa^{-1}\) Maximum root conductance (per leaf area unit)
\(k_{rh, max}\) \(mmol \cdot s^{-1} \cdot m^{-2} \cdot MPa^{-1}\) Maximum rhizosphere conductance (per leaf area unit)
\(k_{l, max}\) \(mmol \cdot s^{-1} \cdot m^{-2} \cdot MPa^{-1}\) VCleaf_kmax Maximum leaf conductance (per leaf area unit)
\(c_l\), \(d_l\) (unitless), MPa VCleaf_c, VCleaf_d Parameters of the vulnerability curve for leaves
\(c_r\), \(d_r\) (unitless), MPa VCroot_c, VCroot_d Parameters of the vulnerability curve for root xylem
\(c_s\), \(d_s\) (unitless), MPa VCstem_c, VCstem_d Parameters of the vulnerability curve for stem xylem
\(n\), \(\alpha\) Texture-specific parameters of the van Genuchten equation
\(\Psi\) MPa Water potential in a given water compartment
\(\Psi_P\) MPa Turgor water potential in a given water compartment
\(\Psi_S\) MPa Osmotic (solute) water potential in a given water compartment
\(\Psi_{cav}\) MPa Minimum water potential experienced by xylem in previous steps (cavitation)
\(\Psi_{canopy}\) MPa Canopy water potential
\(\Psi_{leaf}\) MPa Leaf water potential
\(\Psi_{rootcrown}\) MPa Water potential at the root crown
\(\Psi_{stem}\) MPa Water potential at the end (highest part) of the stem
\(PLC\) [0-1] Proportion of conductance loss in stem xylem tissue
\(p_{root}\) [0-1] pRootDisc Relative root conductance leading to hydraulic disconnection from a soil layer

B.5 Plant water compartments

Symbol Units R param Description
\(\epsilon_{leaf}\) MPa LeafEPS Modulus of elasticity of leaves
\(\epsilon_{stem}\) MPa StemEPS Modulus of elasticity of symplastic xylem tissue
\(\pi_{0,leaf}\) MPa LeafPI0 Osmotic potential at full turgor of leaves
\(\pi_{0,stem}\) MPa StemPI0 Osmotic potential at full turgor of symplastic xylem tissue
\(RWC\) [0-1] Relative water content
\(RWC_{sym}\) [0-1] Relative water content in the symplasm fraction of a tissue
\(RWC_{apo}\) [0-1] Relative water content in the apoplasm fraction of a tissue
\(V_{segment}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-2}\) Water capacity of a segment (leaf or stem)
\(V_{leaf}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-2}\) Leaf water capacity per leaf area unit
\(V_{sapwood}\) \(m^3 \cdot m^{-2}\) Sapwood water capacity per leaf area unit

Symbol Units R param Description

\(J_{max, 298}\) \(\mu mol electrons \cdot m^{-2} \cdot s^{-1}\) Jmax298 Maximum rate of electron transport at 298K
\(V_{max, 298}\) \(\mu mol CO_2 \cdot s^{-1} \cdot m^{-2}\) Vmax298 Rubisco’s maximum carboxylation rate at 298K
